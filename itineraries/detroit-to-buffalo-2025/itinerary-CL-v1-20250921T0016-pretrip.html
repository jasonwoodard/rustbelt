Dashboard Glossary

Use this reference to decode each metric on the dashboard so you can make confident, in-the-moment decisions during a run.

Total Stores

Shows how many store stops are on the agenda so you can gauge the full workload and pacing for the day.

High

A long day ahead that can be draining if your resources are limited.

Low

A more manageable itinerary that makes it easier to stay focused and on schedule.

Stores Visited

Tracks how many stores have been worked so you can judge progress against the plan and momentum of the run.

High

You're on pace and keeping momentum with the route plan.

Low

A signal you might be falling behind, especially if many stops remain.

Avg. J-Score

Summarizes the average anticipated value for your entire itinerary—the mean of your personal “want-to-go” scores for each store.

High

The route is packed with promising stops that can keep morale high.

Low

You're likely hunting for needles in a haystack, so prioritization becomes critical.

Remaining Posterior μ

Estimates the updated anticipated value of the stores you haven't hit yet, guiding whether to push forward or start wrapping up.

High

The rest of the run still looks highly promising, so keep pressing.

Low

The remaining stops may not offer much upside, signaling it could be time to wind down.

Remaining Uncertainty σ

Shows how unknown the untouched stores still are so you know if surprises—good or bad—are likely.

High

The remaining stores are wild cards—expect the unexpected.

Low

Those stops are predictable, giving you more confidence to stick with the plan.

Current Posterior μ

Captures the best estimate of the current store’s quality, telling you if sticking around is likely to pay off.

High

A green light that the store is living up to its potential—stay engaged.

Low

A hint this stop may be a bust, making a quick exit more appealing.

Current Uncertainty σ

Measures how confident you are about the current store’s read so you know whether to trust the estimate.

High

You still don’t have enough intel—keep collecting data.

Low

The read is solid, so it’s easier to make a decisive call.

Current Upper Confidence Bound (UCB) Score

Blends the current store’s quality and risk so you understand the upside if you keep working it right now.

High

There’s strong potential for a big find, making it worth more of your time.

Low

The upside is limited, so moving on could be the smarter play.

Remaining Upper Confidence Bound (UCB) Score

Combines the expected quality and risk of the untouched stores to show the upside left in the route.

High

The rest of the itinerary still has major potential, so stay aggressive.

Low

Limited upside remains, which could be a cue to wrap the day early.
